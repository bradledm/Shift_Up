Our Mission:

To provide a peer to peer learning environment, for IT students, employees, and the enthusiast, To work on hardware and software found in IT departments supporting public and private companies.

  • Who Are We?

    • SandBaux is a Company comprised of IT Professionals who goal is to explore the world of technology, while improving, creating, and helping others reach their IT goals. Our various background in the world of IT includes Automotive, Pharmacetuical, Medical, Wholesale, and commercial. This gives us an insight on how technology drives these industries and what we can do to aid IT professionals enter into the workplace fully understanding what is involved. We are not a school. We are a lab. (We like to think of ourselves as an IT gym.)

  • What We Do?

    • SandBaux provides an environment for anyone wanting to get into the technology field to learn and work on equipment physically and virtually. We aid in preparing those new to IT to learn what physical equipment and software is. We also help those who are already established in their IT careers to continue educating themselves by getting certified in the disciplines of their interest. SandBaux also provides a place for businesses to test out new technologies without impacting their current live working environments.

  • What is Peer Amazing?

    • Peer Amazing is our peer to peer learning initiative. What is peer to peer learning? In our experience we have found that although school the best foundation for theory, the work environment is the fastest way to learn in the field of IT. This is done when employees are trained on the job. Peer amazing our solution to the training ones before entering the workforce. We learn Technology from our peers while having fun, thus peer to peer learning. No pressure, No Grades, just learn and have fun.
